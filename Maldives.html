The Maldives is a tropical paradise known for its stunning coral islands, crystal-clear turquoise waters, and vibrant marine life. Here are some popular sightseeing attractions in the Maldives:

1. Male: The capital city offers a chance to experience Maldivian culture, visit the Maldives Islamic Centre, and explore the local markets, like Malé Fish Market and Malé Local Market.



2. Maafushi Island: A local island that provides an opportunity to experience Maldivian daily life, interact with locals, and enjoy traditional Maldivian cuisine.



3. Thulusdhoo Island: Known for its vibrant art scene, especially for local surfboard shaping and painting. The island is also famous for its local tuna industry.



4. Fihalhohi Island: A beautiful island known for its pristine beaches, clear waters, and vibrant coral reefs.



5. Villingili Island: A local island near Malé with a golf course, historic sites, and a tranquil atmosphere.
