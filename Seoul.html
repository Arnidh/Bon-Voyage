Seoul

The special city

Book now



Seoul, the capital of South Korea, is a vibrant city that seamlessly blends ancient traditions with modern technology. Here are some popular sightseeing attractions in Seoul:

1. Gyeongbokgung Palace: The largest of the Five Grand Palaces built during the Joseon Dynasty, known for its stunning architecture and the changing of the guard ceremony.



2. Bukchon Hanok Village: A preserved traditional Korean village with well-maintained hanok (traditional Korean houses), offering a glimpse into Seoul's history.



3. Myeongdong: A bustling shopping district with a mix of international brands, local boutiques, and street food stalls.



4. Seoul Tower: Located on Namsan Mountain, this iconic tower offers panoramic views of the city, especially beautiful at night.



5. Hongdae: A youthful and artsy district known for its indie music scene, street art, and trendy cafes and shops.



6. Korea National Museum: Showcasing artifacts and exhibits related to Korean history, culture, and art.
