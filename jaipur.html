Jaipur

The Pink City

Book now


Often referred to as the "Pink City" due to the color of its buildings, Jaipur is a popular tourist destination with a wealth of sightseeing attractions. Here are some must-visit places in Jaipur:

1. Hawa Mahal (Palace of Winds): A distinctive landmark of Jaipur, Hawa Mahal is a palace with a unique pink facade featuring intricately designed windows. It was built to allow royal women to observe street festivals without being seen.



2. Amber Fort and Palace: Situated on a hill, Amber Fort is an impressive fortress with stunning views of the surrounding landscape. The fort's Sheesh Mahal (Mirror Palace) is particularly famous for its intricate mirror work.



3. Jantar Mantar: A UNESCO World Heritage Site, Jantar Mantar is an astronomical observatory built by Maharaja Jai Singh II. It features a collection of large-scale instruments used to measure time, predict eclipses, and observe celestial bodies.



4. Jal Mahal: Situated in the middle of the Man Sagar Lake, Jal Mahal is a picturesque palace known for its unique location and architecture. While the palace itself is not open to the public, the view from the shores is captivating.
