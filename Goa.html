Goa, known for its beautiful beaches, vibrant nightlife, and rich cultural heritage, is a popular tourist destination in India. Here are some of the top sightseeing places in Goa:

1. Baga beach â€“ Famous for water sports and lively atmosphere.



2. Fort Aguada - Built by the Portuguese in the 17th century, this fort offers panoramic views of the Arabian Sea and is known for its lighthouse.



3. Calangute beach - Known as the "Queen of Beaches," it's a popular and bustling destination.



4. Basilica of Bom Jesus - A UNESCO World Heritage Site, this church in Old Goa houses the mortal remains of St. Francis Xavier.



5. Dudhsagar Waterfalls - Located in the Bhagwan Mahavir Wildlife Sanctuary, Dudhsagar is one of India's tallest waterfalls. You can reach it by trekking or by taking a jeep safari.
